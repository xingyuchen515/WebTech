My name is Xingyu Chen, an undergraduate student at Babson College. I am majoring in accounting. Last semester, I took a class named "Comic Form in Film." This class enables me to deeply learn the history, development, and techniques of comic forms. Previously I assumed that comic forms can only used in comedy, but after I took that class, I realized that comic forms can be used in romance, animated films, even thrillers. This finding inspired me to watch more genres of movies to see whether comic forms are applied and to watch the movies in different perspectives.
